Slate green is a muted, grayish-green color with subtle variations. Some common variations include:

Light Slate Green

A softer, pastel-like version with more gray and a hint of blue.

Dark Slate Green

A deeper, richer shade with stronger green and gray tones.

Slate Green

A more neutral variant with increased gray, making it more subdued.

Blue Slate Green

A cooler version with a slight bluish undertone.

Olive Slate Green

A warmer shade with hints of yellow or brown.

Muted Slate Green

A desaturated, dusty version, often appearing earthy.

Soft Slate Green

A gentle, airy version with a balanced mix of gray and green.
